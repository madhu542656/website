About WAFES 2K25

WAFES 2K25 is the premier Civil Technical Symposium hosted by the Department of Civil Engineering, Meenakshi Sundararajan Engineering College. Named after the five fundamental elements - Water, Air, Fire, Earth, and Space, WAFES represents the essence of civil engineering and its role in shaping the built environment.

This symposium serves as a dynamic platform where aspiring engineers, researchers, and industry professionals converge to exchange knowledge, showcase innovations, and explore sustainable solutions for the future of construction and infrastructure.

With a series of technical events, workshops, guest lectures, and competitions, WAFES 2K25 aims to ignite creativity, foster collaboration, and push the boundaries of engineering excellence.
